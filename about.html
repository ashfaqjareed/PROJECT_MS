About Multi Super

Your neighbourhood supermarket since 2018.

Our Story

Multi Super began with a simple goal — to give the people of Akurana a modern, well-stocked supermarket right at their doorstep. Founded in 2018 by a group of local entrepreneurs, our first store on Main Street quickly became the go-to shopping destination for families in the Kandy district.

As word spread, so did our reach. We opened our Kandy City branch on Peradeniya Road, followed by Matale and most recently our Wattala outlet — bringing us into the Western Province. Today, Multi Super operates across 4 locations, each tailored to the needs of the local community it serves.

But no matter how much we grow, we never forget where we started. Our commitment is to provide fresh produce, fair prices, and friendly service — every single day. That's the Multi Super promise.

Why Shop With Us

Fresh Every Day

We source produce directly from Central Province farms and local fisheries, ensuring that what you buy today was harvested yesterday.

  • Daily delivery from local farms in Kandy & Matale
  • In-store butcher and bakery at selected branches
  • Cold-chain logistics for dairy and frozen goods
