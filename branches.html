Getting Your Free Loyalty Card

Simply visit the billing counter at any of our branches: Akurana, Katugastota, Warakamura, Mallawapitiya, or Wattala. Ask the cashier to sign you up for a new Loyalty Card. It's completely free!

We only require your active mobile phone number to register an account for you. Your phone number acts as your unique account ID, allowing you to sign into this website and check your balance later.

Present your Loyalty Card (or tell the cashier your registered phone number) before paying your bill. You'll automatically earn 1 point for every Rs. 100 spent in-store.
